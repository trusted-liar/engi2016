Short film making competition, where the theme will be provided a month before engifest. Handpicked Entries will be screened at the venue with the presence of the judges.
Date: 14 Feb
Time: 12:00 pm
Venue: Edusat Hall

Time Limit: 5 to 15 minutes
Theme: Coincidence (Theme is open to interpretation)
