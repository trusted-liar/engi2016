1. Performance should be in Hindi or English or both. However short sub - passages in other languages are allowed .

5. Backstage voiceovers and narrations are allowed during the performance.

12. Inappropriate content, blasphemy or content defaming any political or religious group is strictly prohibited.
13. Time Limit should be strictly followed. Participants will be penalized by judges for not doing so. The time duration for prelims and finals includes performance time and stage setting time. The given time duration is from empty stage to empty stage.
14. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
