They say the best things in life come in threes. If you three think you're one of those things, you have the stage. Show us your magic.
Participants can register both online and offline (on the spot). Only one person from the team should register.
Date: 15 Feb
Time: 10:00 am
Venue: Convocation Hall
1. Time limit: 8 minutes, including set up.
2. There should be exactly three members in a team.
3. The three members have the freedom to sing and/or play any number of instruments.
4. A synthesizer, an acoustic guitar and a drum kit shall be provided. Participants have to bring any other instrument(s) if required.
5. Digital effects processors are allowed, however, use of loopers and digital rhythm patterns are strictly prohibited.
6. Beat boxing is allowed.
Judging Criteria Originality, creativity, coordination and overall impact.
