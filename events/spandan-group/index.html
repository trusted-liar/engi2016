Dancing together is different feet moving to the same beats. So move your feet in synergy and leave a lasting impression on the audience. We invite you to a fleeting moment where you feel alive, to enchant the crowd with your vibrant rhythm and to claim the Spandan crown.
Date: 14th Feb
Time: 12:00 pm
Venue: BR Ambedkar Auditorium
Fees: 300 / team
1. There will be two rounds The first one will be video shortlisting and the second will be the final performance on the campus premises.
2. There is no restriction on the theme. It's an Open theme Group Dance Competition.
3. Maximum 1 team per college allowed.
4. Decision of the judges and the Organising Committee will be final and binding.
5. No responsibility will be held by DTU for any late or lost entries or any such events.
6. Team size should not be more than 20. However there must be at least 6 members on stage at any point during the performance.
7.Time limit per team is 8-10 minutes. Teams will be penalized in case the time limit is exceeded.
8. It is mandatory for all the team members to carry their college ID cards to the final event.
9. Usage of props is allowed and should be consulted with any of the event heads in advance.Use of fire or any other inflammable object is restricted.
10. No lead dancer or solo performance is allowed at any point of time during the performance.
11. Decision of the judges and the Organising Committee will be final and binding. The college reserves the right, in its sole discretion, to disqualify any entry at any time in the event if it is determined that the entrant has not complied with these rules or has acted in an unsportsmanlike or disruptive manner.
