Register | Engifest 2016

For Android Users

Instructions:

Step 1: Download and register on IAMIN App

Step 2: Go to Engifest in the App, claim your free gatepass and click on IAMIN

Step 3: Go to Offers section in the App and download the Loudshout App

Step 4: Register on Loudshout App and select DTU as your basecamp

Entry point verification:

a) QR code scan of ticket received in the IAMIN App or Email received from IAMIN

b) Physical verification of Loudshout App download with DTU base camp selection

For Non-Android Users

Instructions:

Step 1: Visit IAMIN website

Step 2: Complete registration on the IAMIN website and you will receive a registration email

Step 3: Go to hyperlink in the email and download the Loudshout App

Step 4: Register on Loudshout App and select DTU as your basecamp

Entry point verification:

a) Email received from IAMIN

b) Physical verification of Loudshout App download with DTU base camp selection
